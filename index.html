The Ticket Tool for Lambda students.

DevDesk Queue is a platform helping students to address their doubts and queries during their time at Lambda School.

What you can do with DevDesk Queue

Everything you need to address your question and get the issue resolved.

Easy interaction

Quick and easy tickets submission make interaction with Lambda staff more efficient.

Better experience

Communication with support is seamless and efficient - which means more time can be spent on learning.

Categorize

DevDesk Queue allows students to categorize tickets to reduce problem solving time.
